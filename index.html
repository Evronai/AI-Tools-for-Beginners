✨ Best Practices for AI Beginners

Small changes in how you prompt completely change output quality [citation:6]:

1. Start with ONE task

Don't throw everything at AI at once. Pick one workflow, test it, then scale up [citation:6].

❌ "Help me with everything"
✅ "Draft a customer email about our new product"

2. Provide clear context

Give details about audience, tone, and goal. Upload relevant documents [citation:6].

❌ "Write a marketing email"
✅ "Write a short, friendly email for SaaS startups launching analytics dashboard"

3. Use precise steps

Break tasks into numbered steps. Define the AI's role [citation:6].

❌ "Analyze this data"
✅ "Act as marketing strategist. 1. Identify 5 trends 2. Summarize 3. Suggest next steps"

4. Ask for multiple options

Get variations and choose the best one. Politeness actually gets better responses! [citation:6]

❌ "Write a subject line"
✅ "Give me 10 subject lines mixing playful and professional tones"

5. Always fact-check

AI can hallucinate – generate false information confidently. Verify with authoritative sources [citation:4].

⚠️ OpenAI's GPT-5 reduced inaccuracies by 45%, but still not perfect [citation:1]

6. Set data boundaries

Never upload sensitive personal data, company confidential info, or unpublished materials [citation:6].

✓ Use generic examples for testing
✗ Don't upload customer lists or financials
